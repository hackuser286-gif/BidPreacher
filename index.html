Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how BidPreacher works.

Do you process payments?

No. BidPreacher never processes payments. We forward your interest to the operator who connects you with the seller to finalize payment and delivery offline.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We only use your details to connect you with the seller. We don’t sell data or run ads. See our Privacy Policy.

How long until I hear back?

Typically within a few hours during business days. You will receive a WhatsApp message or an email from the operator.

Can I request bulk orders?

Absolutely. Many sellers support bulk orders. Mention your quantity in the notes during checkout.

What if a product is out of stock?

We’ll propose alternatives or notify you when it’s available again.

Do you ship internationally?

Shipping is handled by sellers. International availability depends on the seller’s policy.

Can I return a product?

Returns and warranties are handled by the seller. We’ll help coordinate if needed.

How do I become a seller?

Use the Seller form above. We’ll get in touch to verify details and list your products.

Privacy Policy

Plain-English overview. Detailed policy below.

What we collect

When you tap Buy Now, we collect your name, phone, email, and shipping details to forward your request to the operator. We also store your cart and likes in your browser using localStorage.

How we use it

We only use your details to connect you with the seller—nothing else. No third-party trackers. No ads. No reselling of data.

Data retention

LocalStorage data stays in your browser until you clear it. Requests sent via WhatsApp/Email are handled by the operator and seller per their policies.

Your choices

You may clear your cart and likes anytime. For request copies or deletion, contact us via email.

Terms of Use

Simple terms that keep everyone on the same page.

Service nature

BidPreacher is a connector. We don’t sell products, process payments, or guarantee stock. We forward requests to connect buyers and sellers.

Responsibilities

Product quality, warranties, and deliveries are handled by sellers. We facilitate introductions and support where possible.

Acceptable use

Don’t use this site for illegal items or abusive behavior. We reserve the right to refuse listings and remove content that violates these terms.

Liability
